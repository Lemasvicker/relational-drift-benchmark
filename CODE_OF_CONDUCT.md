# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple community standard:

## Be respectful
Treat others with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

## Be collaborative
Assume good intent, focus on the work, and offer constructive feedback.

## Be safe and responsible
This repo relates to mental-health contexts. Do not post real user data, personal crises, or identifying information.
Do not add prompts that include self-harm methods or instructions.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove contributions or restrict participation if these guidelines are violated.
